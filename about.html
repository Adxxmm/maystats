About the Department of State

Established in 2020, the Mayflower Department of State is responsible for foreign relations, the regulation of domestic businesses and property, and the upkeep of State Archives.

About the State Statistics Office

The State Statistics Office is tasked with accurately managing, sorting and delivering data to the Mayflower public. This data is ensured to be accurate, and is released in a timely manner.
